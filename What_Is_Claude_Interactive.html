Section 01
What Is Claude?
Let's cut through the noise and explain this in words that actually make sense.
Think of it like this

Claude is like a super-smart assistant that has read millions of books, reports, and websites, learned various things, and can instantly apply what it knows to help you with almost any task. It can write, research, analyse, summarise, generate images, write code, and more, all based on what you ask it. So apparently, these are super machines that can learn to do anything.

What Makes Claude Different?

📧

It Connects to Your Business Tools

Claude doesn't just chat. Through Cowork, it plugs into Gmail, Slack, Google Drive, DocuSign and more. It can read your emails, reply to Slack messages, pull files from Drive, and run these tasks on a schedule. No other AI chatbot does this as seamlessly.

📊

It Actually Does the Work

Other AI tools give you answers. Claude gives you finished deliverables. Tell it to create a competitor analysis and it will research, compare pricing, build the spreadsheet, and save it to your desktop. You come back to completed work, not a chat response.

🔁

It Runs Without You

Set up a task once and Claude runs it daily, weekly, or monthly on autopilot. Morning email briefings, weekly competitor reports, monthly expense summaries. Your business keeps moving even when you're not at your desk.

📚

It Reads Entire Contracts in Seconds

Claude can process 200,000+ words in one go. Upload a 100 page contract, a full business plan, or a year's worth of meeting notes and ask it to summarise, flag risks, or pull out key actions. Other tools choke on documents this large.

🎯

It Gets Things Right

Claude is built to hallucinate less than competitors. That means fewer made up facts, fewer wrong numbers, fewer embarrassing mistakes in client-facing documents. When your reputation is on the line, accuracy is everything.

🧩

Skills and Plugins Give It Superpowers

Claude comes with pre-built Skills for creating documents, presentations, spreadsheets and more. Businesses can also build custom Plugins tailored to their specific workflows, turning Claude into a specialist for any department.

AI Agents & Agentic Systems

The easiest way to understand an AI agent? Think about a human customer service agent you'd hire for your business. Then look at what the AI version looks like.

👤

Human Customer Service Agent

The person you'd hire

🧠
Has a Mind

Their brain. Thinks, reasons, makes judgements based on experience and training.

🔧
Has Tools

Computer, phone, CRM software, email, spreadsheets. Uses these to get the job done.

📖
Has a Knowledge Base

Company handbook, FAQs, product docs, pricing sheets. The information they reference.

💾
Has Memory

Remembers past conversations, customer history, previous issues and resolutions.

⚡
Has Autonomy

Can make decisions on their own. Issue a refund, escalate a complaint, offer a discount.

🎯
Has Roles & Objectives

Job description, KPIs, targets. "Resolve 90% of tickets within 24 hours."

🤖

AI Customer Service Agent

The machine equivalent

🧠
Has a Mind (The Model)

The AI model (Claude, GPT, Gemini). Thinks, reasons, and generates responses based on training data.

🔧
Has Tools

APIs, web search, Gmail, Slack, databases, file systems. Connects to the same tools, just digitally.

📖
Has a Knowledge Base

Company docs, product info, FAQs uploaded or connected. The agent retrieves what it needs in milliseconds.

💾
Has Memory

Stores conversation history, customer preferences, past interactions. Picks up where it left off.

⚡
Has Autonomy

Can make decisions within set rules. Process a refund, route a ticket, send a follow up. All without asking you.

🎯
Has Roles & Objectives

System prompt, guardrails, success metrics. "Resolve customer issues, escalate if unsure, never promise what we can't deliver."

So what's the difference?

They do the same job. The AI version is just a machine. It's faster (handles 1,000 conversations at once), tends to be more accurate at repetitive tasks (never has a bad day), works 24/7 (no holidays, no sick leave), and costs a fraction of a salary. The human brings empathy, nuance, and creativity that AI is still catching up on. The smartest businesses use both together.

Section 02
Anthropic Owns Claude
Three products, three purposes, each built for different people and use cases.
💬

Claude AI

Web & Mobile | More for Personal Use

The main chatbot on claude.ai and the mobile app. Type a question, get an answer. Great for writing, research, analysis, brainstorming, and daily tasks.

Strengths
  • Easy to use, just type and chat
  • Web search, file uploads, memory
  • Available on web, desktop & mobile
Limitations
  • Free tier has usage limits
  • Can't run code on your computer
  • No local file access
🤝

Claude Cowork

Desktop Based | More for Business Purposes

Launched Jan 2026. Automates workflows, increases productivity. Accesses your local files, creates docs, organises folders, runs scheduled tasks. Connects to Gmail, Google Drive, DocuSign.

Strengths
  • No coding required
  • Creates Excel, PowerPoint, docs
  • Automates recurring tasks
  • Connects Gmail, Drive, DocuSign
Limitations
  • Still in research preview
  • Desktop app required
  • Computer must stay awake
⌨️

Claude Code

Operates in the Terminal | For Software Development

A command-line tool for developers. Reads codebases, writes features, fixes bugs, reviews code. Grew to a billion-dollar product in 6 months.

Strengths
  • Best-in-class code generation
  • Reads entire codebases
  • Finds & fixes bugs autonomously
Limitations
  • Requires technical knowledge
  • Command-line only
  • Not for non-coding tasks
In plain English

Claude AI = texting a smart friend (web/phone). Claude Cowork = a virtual employee on your desktop who does actual work. Claude Code = a coding partner for devs in the terminal.

Section 03
ChatGPT, Gemini & Claude: The Big Three
All three are super-intelligent chatbots with similarities and exceptional capabilities, built by different companies. They can all create/generate text, images, summarise and analyse things. They're built on machine learning models that can learn, think, and do tasks. Like three brilliant minds with similar skills but different strengths.
🟢

ChatGPT

by OpenAI · launched Nov 2022
"The Popular All-Rounder"

The most widely used AI chatbot. Best known for creative content, brainstorming, and general-purpose tasks. Has the largest user community and plugin ecosystem. Think of it as the Swiss Army knife, good at many things.

Key Strengths
  • Huge community & plugin store (GPTs)
  • Strong creative writing & brainstorming
  • Image generation (DALL-E)
  • Memory across conversations
  • Versatile all-rounder
🟠

Claude

by Anthropic · launched Mar 2023
"The Careful, Deep Thinker"

The most accurate and careful of the three. Excels at long analysis, polished writing, and coding. Key differentiator: Skills, Plugins, and external tool connections (Gmail, Slack, Drive, DocuSign) via Cowork, making it the most business-action-ready AI.

Key Strengths
  • Best writing quality & analysis
  • Fewest hallucinations (most accurate)
  • Skills: pre-built abilities for specific tasks
  • Plugins & connectors (Gmail, Slack, Drive+)
  • Cowork: acts on your files & workflows
  • Top coding benchmarks
🔵

Gemini

by Google · launched Dec 2023
"The Fast, Connected Researcher"

Google's AI, deeply tied into their ecosystem. Best for quick research, real-time web info, and if your business already runs on Google Workspace. Think of it as the researcher with a direct line to the internet.

Key Strengths
  • Real-time web access built in
  • Seamless Google Workspace integration
  • Excellent multimodal (text+image+video)
  • Fastest response times
  • Best free tier of the three
